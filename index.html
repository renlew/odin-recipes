Bacon, Egg and Chive Quiche

Time Estimate: 1 hr

Ingredients

  • 1 9" Pastry/Pie Crust
  • 7 Eggs
  • 4 Bacon
  • 3/4 c Cottage Chesse
  • 3 Chives
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Onion Powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 fahrenheit.
  2. Pan fry the bacon for 15 minutes then place on napkin to drain grease.
  3. In the meantime, combine the eggs and cottage cheese in a bowl, don't worry the cottage cheese won't be lumpy once cooked.
  4. Chop the chives, seperating the whites from the greens.
  5. Once bacon is done cooking, coarsely chop and add to the mixture with the chive whites.
  6. Once all the ingredients have been added to the bowl put in the spices and give it one final mix.
  7. Add ingredients to pie crust and place in the oven to bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Once the quiche is browned and the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees fahrenheit, let it cool for 10 minutes then top with green chives.
  9. Eat up!
